# CR-003 – HJ-003 Ubiquitous Language Guide

## Document

**HJ-003 – Ubiquitous Language Guide**

## Version

Update to **Version 1.2**

## Objective

Replace the Trading Model concept with Trading Characteristics and introduce the terminology required to support Vendor Registration, Compliance and controlled Registered Information.

## Changes

### 1. Remove Trading Model

Remove the existing **Trading Model** term and all associated references.

### 2. Introduce Trading Characteristics

Add a new business concept:

**Trading Characteristics**

Trading Characteristics describe the characteristics of a Vendor's trading operation that are used to determine compliance requirements.

Trading Characteristics consist of:

- Trading Location
- Opening Hours
- Service Includes Hot Food
- Alcohol Service

### 3. Add Trading Location

Add the following controlled values.

- Customer-Facing Permanent Premises
- Mobile Food Unit or Market Stall
- Home or Dark Kitchen

Include descriptions consistent with the Vendor Domain Models.

### 4. Add Registered Information

Define:

**Registered Information**

The information supplied during Vendor Registration that establishes the legal identity and operating characteristics of a Vendor.

Registered Information includes:

- Trading Name
- Legal Operator Name
- Legal Operator Type
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Name
- Contact Email
- Contact Telephone
- Business Address
- Trading Characteristics

### 5. Add Vendor Managed Information

Define:

**Vendor Managed Information**

Information that may be maintained directly by the Vendor without affecting the registered identity of the business.

Examples include:

- Website
- Business Description

### 6. Add Business Rule

Add the following business rule.

Registered Information becomes read-only to the Vendor once Vendor Registration has been successfully submitted and the Vendor enters PendingActivation.

Changes to Registered Information are performed only by authorised platform operators using administrative processes.

### 7. Update Glossary

Update glossary entries affected by the above changes.

### 8. Update Revision History

Record Version 1.2 changes.

## Preservation Requirements

- Preserve all existing numbering.
- Preserve all existing formatting.
- Preserve all existing content unless changed above.
